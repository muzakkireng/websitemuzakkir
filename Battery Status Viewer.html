Check Your Device's Battery Status Online

Our free Battery Status Viewer tool allows you to monitor your device's battery information in real-time. Using the modern web Battery Status API, we provide accurate details about your battery level, charging status, and estimated time remaining.

How Our Battery Status Checker Works

When you visit this page, your browser requests permission to access your device's battery information. The tool then displays:

  • Battery Percentage - The current charge level of your device
  • Charging Status - Whether your device is currently charging or discharging
  • Time Remaining - Estimated time until fully charged or discharged
  • Battery Health - General indication of your battery's condition

Why Monitor Your Battery Status?

Regularly checking your battery status can help you:

  • Plan your work sessions around charging needs
  • Identify potential battery health issues early
  • Optimize your device's power settings
  • Extend your battery's overall lifespan

Browser Compatibility

Our Battery Status Viewer works with most modern browsers including Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Opera. Note that some browsers may restrict this API for security reasons, and Safari currently doesn't support it.

Privacy Information

Your battery information is processed locally in your browser and never sent to our servers. We respect your privacy and don't store any personal data through this tool.
