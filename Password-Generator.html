Why Strong Passwords Matter

In today's digital world, strong passwords are your first line of defense against cyber attacks. Weak passwords are responsible for over 80% of data breaches.

A strong password should be unique for each account, long (12+ characters), and include a mix of character types. Our generator creates passwords that meet all security requirements.

Creating Secure Passwords

  • Use at least 12 characters with a mix of uppercase, lowercase, numbers and symbols
  • Avoid personal information like names, birthdays or addresses
  • Never reuse passwords across multiple accounts
  • Consider using a password manager for secure storage
  • Enable two-factor authentication whenever possible
