Frequently Asked Questions

What counts as a word?

Any sequence of characters separated by spaces is counted as a word. Numbers and symbols are included in the word count.

How are characters counted?

Every single character including letters, numbers, spaces, and punctuation marks is counted. We also provide character count without spaces.

What defines a sentence?

A sentence is defined as a sequence of words ending with a period, exclamation mark, or question mark.

Is there a limit to how much text I can analyze?

No, there are no limits! You can analyze as much text as you need. Our tool is designed to handle large documents efficiently.
