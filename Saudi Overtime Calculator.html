Saudi Overtime Regulations

Legal Framework

Saudi Labor Law (Royal Decree No. M/51) regulates overtime compensation. According to Article 107, employers must compensate overtime work at specific premium rates based on the type of day and time of work.

Night Shift Premium

Work performed between 10 PM and 6 AM is considered night shift and entitles employees to an additional 30% of their basic hourly wage on top of the regular overtime rate.

Important Notes

• Daily working hours should not exceed 12 hours including overtime
• Friday is the official weekly rest day in Saudi Arabia
• Overtime must be voluntary and compensated accordingly
• These rates apply to private sector employees
