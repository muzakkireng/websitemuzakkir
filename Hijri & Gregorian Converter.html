Understanding Hijri and Gregorian Calendars

The Hijri calendar is a lunar calendar used in many Muslim countries for religious purposes. It has 12 months and 354 or 355 days. The Gregorian calendar is the internationally accepted civil calendar with 365 days (366 in leap years). Our converter ensures accurate translation between these two systems.
