Why Website Speed Matters

User Experience

53% of visitors abandon sites that take longer than 3 seconds to load. Faster sites lead to lower bounce rates and higher engagement.

SEO Rankings

Google uses page speed as a ranking factor. Faster websites rank higher in search results, driving more organic traffic.

Conversion Rates

A 1-second delay can reduce conversions by 7%. Faster sites improve user trust and increase sales.

Mobile Optimization

With over 60% of web traffic from mobile devices, optimizing for mobile speed is crucial for reaching your audience.
